University of San Diego, School of Law

San Diego, CA

University of San Diego, School of Law is located in San Diego, CA and enrolls approximately 240 first-year (1L) students. The median LSAT is 163 and median GPA is 3.84. In the most recent reporting year, 90% of graduates secured full-time, bar-required employment, with 17.7% at large firms (251+ attorneys) and 1.2% in federal clerkships.
